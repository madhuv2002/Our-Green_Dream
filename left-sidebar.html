About Us

Jessica

Jessica Hernandez is an incoming freshman at the University of Florida and is excited to study Computer Science at the College of Liberal Arts and Sciences. She enjoys reading, writing book reviews, rock climbing, and cooking with her best friend. Jessica has been vegan for over six months and loves teaching others about veganism and inspiring others to make changes that ultimately help the environment. She also has a passion for journalism and hopes to combine it with her love for computer science in the future.

Caroline

Caroline Dugan is currently a junior at Highland Park High School and will graduate in 2020. Her hobbies include running on the HP Cross Country team, working at The Pilates Barre, and traveling around the world with new people on a program called Rustic Pathways. Caroline is starting a local chapter of Girls Who Code at Highland Park High School and is excited to help local girls start learning how to code and why they should think about studying computer science. College is still undecided but California would be the ideal place to spread our Green Dream even further.

Madhu

Madhumitha Vakkadala is an incoming junior at Coppell High School in Dallas, TX. She enjoys volunteering and reading books in her free time, and is excited to learn more about coding and computer science. She is a part of her school’s table tennis team, and also enjoys playing other sports like basketball, badminton, and swimming. She is also a part of the National Honor Society and a member of her school’s Science Olympiad team.

Na’Quaiviera

Na’Quaiviera is currently a freshman at Hillcrest High School in Dallas, Texas and is attending their Collegiate Academy program and is majoring in Pre-Engineering. Her hobbies are dancing, singing, coding, cooking, and playing sports. Na’Quaiviera is also a pre graphic designer and artist. She was born and raised from Milwaukee, Wisconsin.
